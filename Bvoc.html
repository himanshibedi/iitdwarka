Bachelor in Voccation (B.Voc)

Introduction

The University Grants Commission (UGC) has launched a scheme on skills development based higher education as part of college/university education, leading to Bachelor of Vocation (B.Voc.) Degree with multiple exit and entry option to a Diploma/Advanced Diploma or Bachelors Degree, under the NSQF (National Skills Qualifications Framework). The course content is developed based on NVEQF (National Vocational Educational Qualification Framework), NSQF, CBCS (Choice Based Credit System), Industry requirements and achieves the following objectives in mind. To develop a wholistic personality by imparting human centric learning. To impart skill based education. To promote Entrepreneurship among students This programme is designed to cater the demands of professionally trained human resource in the field of Software Development. The programme is highly relevant for all those who want to pursue a professional career in software development.

Vision

To maintain academic excellence aiming to produce competent technocrats imbibed with ethical values to serve the nation emerging technological needs.
